I'm sure you've heard about it by now - over 10000 dead in a massive natural disaster. No point repeating the news. But, I want to do my part to make people aware that they can help. Many major international aid agencies have set up donation schemes online, so don't just sit there - click on over to one (or more!) of these:

  • The Australian Red Cross
  • CARE Australia
  • Oxfam Australia

It's like they're always trying to tell us - you can make a difference.
