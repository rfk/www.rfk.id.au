Sometimes burrowing through your site statistics is just a little too disturbing. One particular phrase that turned up in my list of "top ten search terms" took me a little by surprise. The effect is best demonstrated by the following procedure:

  1. Go to http://www.google.com.au/
  2. Select the "pages from Australia" button
  3. Enter the query "her breasts sagged", including the quotes
  4. Observe that the top-rated result points to the RFK.id.au domain.

Perhaps the madness will stop when I work out how to seperate Sazz's site statistics from my own :-)
