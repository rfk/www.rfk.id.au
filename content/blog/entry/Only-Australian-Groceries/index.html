It's amazing the things you can discover when you're supposed to be studying. Only Australian Groceries is an on-line supermarket dedicated to stocking only Australian-owned and Australian-made products. There are a lot of products listed that I have never seen on supermarket shelves, more's the pity. Interestingly, delivery is made using the standard Australia Post parcel service.

Sadly, I dont think even I'm ready for online grocery shopping just yet, especially since I still need to pop across to BiLo for milk. However, I've already found it to be a great guide to which products to buy in a regular bricks-and-mortar supermarket.
