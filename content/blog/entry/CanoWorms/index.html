Last week I finally got around to something I've been considering for more than two years now - Lozz and I set up a worm farm in our back yard.

It's a Can-O-Worms model, designed and built right here in Australia from 100% recycled plastic. Once the worms are properly established, they should easily be able to consume all of our organic waste and the end product will keep our fast-growing veggie garden in tip-top shape.

There's not much action in the farm at the moment, I think the worms are still a little shell-shocked after their transplanation. But if you want to see what it should look like when the action starts, check out the photos on this PetWorms Blog.
